Annotation guidelines
  • Answer based only on the passage — do not use outside knowledge.
  • Answer Yes if the passage provides direct evidence the statement is true of the person asked about.
  • Answer No if the passage contradicts the statement, or the evidence applies to a different person than the one being asked about.
  • If unsure, go with your best reading of what the passage says.
Example — Yes
